About Roto-Rooter Plumbing & Water Cleanup

Roto-Rooter has been a trusted name in Huntsville plumbing since 1963, operating as a family-owned franchise for over 60 years. They are one of the most recognizable plumbing brands in the country, and the Huntsville location combines that national reputation with deep local roots in the community.

Their experienced technicians provide full-service plumbing solutions including drain cleaning, sewer line repair, water damage restoration, toilet and faucet repair, water heater services, and emergency plumbing repairs. They are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year for both residential and commercial customers throughout Huntsville and Madison County.

Customers consistently praise Roto-Rooter for their professionalism, prompt response times, and fair pricing. Their BBB A+ accreditation since 1973 speaks to their longstanding commitment to customer satisfaction.
