About B & C Plumbing, Inc.

B & C Plumbing is a locally owned and operated plumbing company with over 30 years of combined commercial and residential plumbing experience. Established in 2001 and managed by President Donald Towry, the company has grown into one of Huntsville's most respected plumbing operations.

Their team of over 20 master and journeyman plumbers handles a full spectrum of services including new construction plumbing, remodeling, residential service calls, line locating, TVI line camera inspections, water heater services, and emergency repairs. They offer same-day service, free project estimates, and maintain a real person available 24/7 to answer calls.

B & C Plumbing holds a BBB A+ rating and is accredited since 2007. They are licensed under the State of Alabama Plumbers & Gas Fitters Examining Board and are consistently praised for their professional, efficient, and courteous service.
