About Scott Plumbing LLC

Scott Plumbing is a family-owned business led by Keith Scott, dedicated to providing personalized plumbing solutions throughout Huntsville and Madison County. Since opening in 2014, they have rapidly built one of the strongest reputations in the area, earning the title of Best of Huntsville Plumbers two years in a row.

They specialize in residential service including repair and replacement of all fixtures, water heaters, pipe replacement, and leak detection. Every technician on their team is drug-tested and background checked, giving homeowners peace of mind when inviting them into their homes. They also offer same-day service with a technician dispatched to your home as quickly as possible.

Scott Plumbing holds a BBB A+ rating and has received the Angi and HomeAdvisor Super Service Award. Their 4.8-star average across over 1,500 reviews is a testament to their commitment to integrity, dedication, and care in every job they perform.
