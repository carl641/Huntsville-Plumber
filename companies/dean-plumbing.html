About Dean Plumbing Co., Inc.

Dean Plumbing has been delivering exceptional plumbing and gas piping services to the Huntsville community since 1954, making them one of the most established and experienced plumbing companies in all of North Alabama with over 70 years of industry expertise.

Run by the Dean family under the leadership of President Michael Dean, the company handles both commercial and residential projects. Their services include new plumbing installations, repairs, gas piping, water heater services, drain cleaning, and 24/7 emergency plumbing support for urgent situations.

Dean Plumbing holds a BBB A+ rating and has been accredited since 2007. Their decades of experience and deep community ties have made them a go-to choice for homeowners and businesses looking for reliable, professional plumbing service in the Huntsville metro area.
