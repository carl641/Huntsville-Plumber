About Hutchens Company

The Hutchens Company is one of Huntsville's longest-standing home service providers, founded in 1886 and now operated by the fourth generation of the Hutchens family. With over 130 years of continuous service to the North Alabama community, they bring an unmatched depth of experience and local knowledge to every project.

Their services span both plumbing and HVAC, including drain cleaning, tankless water heater maintenance and installation, gas and water pipe replacement, fixture repair, and comprehensive heating and cooling solutions. Their NATE-certified technicians are available for scheduled appointments as well as on-call for urgent situations.

Hutchens Company's deep roots in the Huntsville community and their multigenerational commitment to quality workmanship make them a trusted choice for homeowners who value experience, reliability, and a company that truly knows the area they serve.
