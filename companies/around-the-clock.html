About Around the Clock Plumbing & Septic

Around the Clock Plumbing & Septic is a family-owned and operated business with nearly 100 years of combined experience in the septic and plumbing industry. They have been proudly serving Huntsville, Alabama, and the surrounding communities with reliable, professional plumbing and septic services.

True to their name, they provide 24/7 emergency services for urgent plumbing needs including leak repair, sewage cleaning, pipe repair, and burst pipe response. Their full range of services also includes septic pumping, cleaning, and installation, water heater repair and installation, drain cleaning, and general plumbing maintenance.

BBB accredited since 2017, Around the Clock Plumbing has built a solid reputation for responsive service and fair pricing. Their commitment to being available whenever plumbing emergencies strike gives Huntsville homeowners peace of mind around the clock.
