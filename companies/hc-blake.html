About HC Blake / Blake Brothers

HC Blake is Huntsville's oldest home services company, originally founded in 1884 and still family owned and operated by descendants of the original founders. Now operating as Blake Brothers Service Corp, the company has been a cornerstone of the North Alabama community for over 138 years.

They offer a comprehensive range of services including plumbing repair and installation, HVAC replacement and repair, electrical work, gas services, crawlspace solutions, and whole-home preventative maintenance. Their staff brings over 135 years of combined experience to every job.

Blake Brothers holds a BBB A+ rating and their outstanding 4.9-star review average reflects the exceptional quality of service and craftsmanship they consistently deliver. Their longevity in the Huntsville market speaks volumes about the trust the community places in their work.
