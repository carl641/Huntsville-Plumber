About Mr. Rooter Plumbing of Huntsville

Mr. Rooter Plumbing of Huntsville is a locally owned and operated franchise that has been serving residential and commercial properties throughout the Huntsville area since 1990. As part of the Neighborly family of home service brands, they combine the reliability of a national network with the personalized attention of a local business.

Their team of licensed plumbers offers a comprehensive range of services including drain cleaning, sewer line repair and replacement, water heater installation, garbage disposal repair, toilet and faucet repair, and complete plumbing system maintenance. They are available 24/7 for emergency plumbing situations and are known for their transparent, upfront pricing with no overtime charges.

Mr. Rooter stands out for their commitment to customer satisfaction and their guarantee that the job will be done right the first time. They serve Huntsville, Madison, Decatur, and the surrounding North Alabama communities.
